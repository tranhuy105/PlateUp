Game Tutorial

Welcome to Plate Up! Here's a quick guide to get you started:

  • Movement: Use the arrow keys to move your character.
  • Pickup: Press 'P' to pick up items.
  • Drop: Press 'D' to drop items.
  • Process: Press 'O' to process items (like cooking).
  • Reset: Press 'R' to reset the game state in case of something went wrong!.

Recipes

  • Raw Pizza: Processed Tomato + Processed Cheese.
  • Pizza: Raw Pizza drop into oven.
  • Hotdog: Bread + Sausage drop at stove.

Happy cooking!
